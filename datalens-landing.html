How it works

From Excel to insights
in 3 steps

No technical skills required. If you can use Excel, you can use DataLens.

1

Upload your Excel file

Drag and drop any .xlsx, .xls, or .csv file. DataLens automatically detects your columns, identifies numeric data, and prepares everything for visualization. Max 10,000 rows, 5MB.

2

Explore your charts

Your dashboard is ready instantly. Switch between Bar, Line, Area, and Pie charts. Choose which columns to plot on X and Y axis. Filter by any text column to drill into specific segments.

3

Share with your team or clients

Create client accounts so they can log in and view only their data. Export charts as SVG or print to PDF for presentations and reports. Your data stays private and secure.
